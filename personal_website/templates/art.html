Sketching

Sketching will always be a passion of mine. It doesn't have to be on a sketchbook or on a fancy piece of paper, sketching is simply that â€” a rough piece of art. Interestingly, I find them the most appealing. A sketch shows the rough linework or a more defined piece depending on the mindset of the artist at the moment of creation.

It's a calming experience to put pen to paper and an amazing feeling to know that you've improved over the years. Looking back at old sketchbooks, I get to re-experience a part of my life simply through these pen or pencil strokes. No words are needed, only the experience.
